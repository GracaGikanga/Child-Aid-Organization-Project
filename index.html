Together, Protecting Every Child's Future.

Child Aid Organization Kenya (CAOK) is an NGO that supports various ventures that strive to promote to create a better future for children and sustainable growth and development for schools in developing countries.

Our mission is to create a safe environment for every child, free from abuse,neglect and discrimination. Join us in ensuring that all children grow up with dignity, protection and hope for a better tomorrow.

Focus areas

  1. Aiding the disadvantaged children
  2. Curating learning and networking opportunities.
  3. Providing funding and coaching to scale innovations in partnership with governments.
  4. Strategic policy engagement
