Activities of the Organisation

  1. Awareness and advocacy Campaigns

    • Educating the public about child rights and the importance of protecting children from abuse and neglect
    • Organising local and national campaigns like workshops, community meetings and roadshows to raise awareness of child protection laws and reporting mechanisms
    • Advocacy for stronger enforcement of child protection policies and laws.

  2. Reporting and helpline serveices

    • Running a 24/7 child protection helpline where children or community members can report abuse and seek advice
    • Developing mobile apps and online platforms where incidents of abuse can be reported anonymously

  3. Couseling and rehabilitation programs

    • Offereing free counselling or therapy to children who have experienced abuse or poor treatment.
    • Providing rehabilitation programs including mentorship and emotional support, to help children reconcile and reconnect with thr community
