Completed projects

  1. Child Protection Awareness Campaign

    2. Goal: To educate commnuities on recognizing and preventing child abuse

    Activities

    • Posters, flyers and films about child rights and abuse were distributed.
    • A community walk was held in Nairobi to educate on child safety.

  2. Parenting and caregiver workshop

    3. Facilitators: Child psychologists and councelors.

    Goal: To equip parents with skills that are create safe and supportive homes.

    Activities

    • Sessions on positive discipline methods while highlighting and shaming violent methods were conducted.
    • Training on avoiding harmful cultural practices were conducted

    2. Goal:To teach Children how to protect themselves from abuse

Collaborations

    2. Goal: To empower schools as the first line of defense against child abuse

    Activities:
