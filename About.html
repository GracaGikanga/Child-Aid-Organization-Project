Who we are.

CAOK was established in 1955 by a committee led by Sir Humprey Slade, a lawyer who later became the speaker of Kenya's National Assembly.

We are a dedicated organisation committed to prevent and stop cruelty, negligence,and abuse against children as well as all forms of gender-based violence.

What we do

    2. Raising awareness about child rights and abuse prevention.

  2. Support services
